JomPAY

You can pay using JomPAY via online banking with the following steps:

1. Log on to your internet banking and look for JomPAY.

2. Enter 85498 as the Biller Code.

3. Enter your IC number as Ref-1.

4. Enter your IC Name as Ref-2.

5. Enter your Payment Amount.

Direct Debit (e-Mandate)

Register your direct debit account with murahcredit.com. Once the registration is done, all you need to do is to prepare enough funds for a deduction on your repayment date.
